Fish Curry

Fish curry is a flavorful dish made by simmering fish in a rich sauce of spices, tomatoes, and coconut milk or yogurt.

It's often served with rice or bread, offering a delightful blend of tangy, spicy, and savory flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Fish
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Coconut Milk
  1. Prepare the Fish:Clean and cut the fish into pieces.
  2. Cook in Oil:In a pan, heat oil, add turmeric and salt, then pour in coconut milk (or water) and bring to a simmer.
  3. Add Fish:Add the fish pieces, simmer until fully cooked.
  4. Serve:Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
