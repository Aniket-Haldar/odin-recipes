Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is a popular Indian street food dish consisting of a spicy mashed vegetable mix served with buttered, toasted bread rolls (pav).

It's often garnished with onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime, making it a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Cauliflower
  • Green Peas
  • Tomatoes
  • Pav Bhaji masala
  • Pav(bread rolls)
  • Onions

Steps:

  1. Boil and Mash Vegetables:Boil potatoes, cauliflower, and peas until soft, then mash them together.
  2. Cook the Base:In a pan, heat butter and saut√© chopped onions until golden. Add chopped tomatoes, pav bhaji masala, and salt, cooking until the tomatoes soften
  3. Combine:Add the mashed vegetables to the pan, mix well, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Toast Pav:Toast the pav (bread rolls) in butter until golden.
  5. Serve:Serve the bhaji hot with the toasted pav, garnished with coriander and lemon wedges.
